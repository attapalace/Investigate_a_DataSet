Introduction¶

This report will include Data wrangling and analysis for TMDb Movie Dataset and answering questions regarding the movies genres and other variables and in particular:

  • Which genres of movies are most popular
  • What types of genres are most produced?
  • What movie genres are most profitable?
  • Do movies make more profit if they are released in certain month of the year?
  • How do the movies budget and revenue change over the years?
  • What is the Productions Rate over the years?
  • What is the Vote average for all movies?
